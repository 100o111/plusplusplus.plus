This is a holy moment. It is the bond of the faithful and their directors of conscience,

my very own critique of the spectacle,

My interpretation of the modern confession:

This is Your local center of power knowledge,

where spectacle manifests, a space for performance and appearance.

It is a counterpoint, a so-called genuine self-assessment and transformation.
